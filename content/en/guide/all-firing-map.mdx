<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Strategies for Firing Range Domination">
To truly dominate Firing Range, you need to go beyond basic map knowledge. Exploiting hidden spots, understanding movement patterns, and using your loadout effectively will give you the edge.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Crossfire">
The map's center, particularly around the Tower and main Road, is a constant crossfire zone. Unless you have a strong position or a specific plan, prolonged exposure in this area can be lethal. Use cover and movement to your advantage.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Tower Early">
    The Tower is the most coveted position. A well-coordinated team push to secure it early can establish map control. Use smoke grenades to cover your approach and have teammates watch the Garage, Shoot House, and Office entrances. Once secured, use its height advantage for Scorestreaks, but be mindful of enemies pushing from below.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Flanking Routes">
    If the center is too hot, the Dirt Road and Containers offer excellent flanking opportunities. The Dirt Road allows you to circumvent the main action and potentially catch enemies rotating from the Depot or Shoot House. The Containers area provides cover and access to the Bunker or Garage, allowing for unexpected pushes into the center.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Exploit Ambush Points">
    Firing Range is rife with tight corners and small alcoves perfect for ambushes.
    - **Shipping Crate (near Containers)**: A small opening allows an Operator to hide and ambush targets rushing through. Toss a grenade if you suspect an enemy.
    - **Shoot House Roof Opening**: Drop down from the roof opening near the staircase to surprise enemies on the first floor of the Shoot House.
    - **Covered Shed (behind Shoot House)**: Jump on top of this shed for a clear sightline down the Dirt Road, maintaining a height advantage.
    - **Upper Storage (Dirt Road)**: A prime ambush spot against Operators moving along the flanking route.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Map Awareness with Perks">
    Equip perks like **Engineer** or the **Strategist Combat Specialty** to see enemy equipment through walls. This is invaluable on Firing Range, helping you anticipate threats along dangerous routes like the center, Dirt Road, and Containers, giving you a crucial informational advantage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Historical Significance and Map Variations">
Firing Range holds a special place in Call of Duty history, having appeared in multiple titles since its debut in the original Black Ops. Its enduring popularity stems from its balanced design and consistent flow of action.

<Callout type="info" title="Evolution of Firing Range">
The Firing Range map has seen several iterations and remakes across various Call of Duty titles, including its appearance in _Call of Duty Online_, _Black Ops III_, _Black Ops 4_, and _Call of Duty: Mobile_. Each version maintains the core layout while sometimes introducing minor aesthetic or environmental changes, ensuring its timeless appeal.
</Callout>

- **Call of Duty: Black Ops (2010)**: The original introduction, establishing its iconic layout.
- **Call of Duty: Black Ops III (2015)**: Featured as part of the "Back in Black" map pack, exclusive to PlayStation 4 preorders of Black Ops 4.
- **Call of Duty: Black Ops 4 (2018)**: Included as a launch map, also featuring a night variant, "Firing Range Night".
- **Call of Duty: Mobile**: Adapted for mobile play, maintaining its fast-paced nature.
- **Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 (2025)**: A faithful remaster, bringing the classic back with updated graphics.
- **Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 (2026)**: Introduced as part of the Season Two Reloaded update on March 11th, 2026, building upon the BO6 remaster.

The map's design, initially a military training facility, is reflected in its various points of interest like the Shoot House and the target range. Little details, like the "Get Some!" message on a Huey outside the map in the original Black Ops, add to its character and lore.

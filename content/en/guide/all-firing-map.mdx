<Section icon={Zap} title="Advanced Tactical Strategies for 2026">

Moving beyond the basics, high-level play on **all firing map** versions requires utilizing specific environmental "tricks" that many players overlook. The faster movement speed of the 2026 engine makes these maneuvers even more effective.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Crate Ambush">
    Near the Containers, look for a blasted opening in the side of a shipping crate. It is just large enough to hide an Operator. Use this to catch enemies racing toward the center before they even see you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verticality at Shoot House">
    Don't just stay on the stairs. Jump onto the covered shed behind the building to maintain a height advantage while maintaining a clear sightline down the Dirt Road.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Storage Room Clearance">
    When flanking via the Dirt Road, always toss a grenade into the upper Storage room. It is a prime ambush spot for defenders waiting to cut off flankers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tower Counter-Play">
    If an enemy is entrenched in the Tower, use the Bunker overhang to adjust your angles. You can often see their feet before they can see your head.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Equip the **Engineer Perk** or the **Strategist Combat Specialty**. Being able to see enemy equipment through walls is a massive advantage in the cramped interiors of the Office and Shoot House.
</Callout>

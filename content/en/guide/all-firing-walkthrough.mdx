### Mastering Real-Time Switching

Effective character switching is the backbone of ALLfiring's combat system. It allows you to extend combos, mitigate damage, and deploy specific skills when needed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Observe Enemy Patterns">
    Before engaging aggressively, take a moment to watch enemy attack animations and telegraphs. This helps you anticipate their moves and plan your switches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Chain Abilities Seamlessly">
    Switch between characters to use their skills back-to-back, creating longer combo chains and maximizing damage output. For example, use a crowd control ability with one character, then switch to a high-DPS character for burst damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Dodges and Ultimates">
    If a character is about to take heavy damage, switch them out and use another character to dodge or absorb the hit. Save Ultimates for boss encounters or when you need to clear multiple enemies quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Management">
    Keep an eye on each character's skill cooldowns and energy. Switch to characters with available abilities while others are on cooldown, ensuring continuous combat flow.
  </Step>

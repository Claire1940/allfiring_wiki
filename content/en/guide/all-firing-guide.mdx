Once you have mastered the basics of this **all firing guide**, you can start implementing advanced tactics to clear difficult content like the Shadow Abyss.

**Perfect Dodging:**
Timed dodges don't just avoid damage; they trigger a "Bullet Time" effect where enemies move slowly, allowing you to unleash a full combo safely. Practice the timing against the early Shadow Beast bosses.

**Switch Combos:**
When a character uses their Ultimate or a heavy skill, switch to your next character immediately. The previous character will often finish their animation while your new character starts theirs, effectively allowing two characters to attack simultaneously for a short window.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Reroll Strategy">
Rerolling in All Firing is straightforward and efficient, making it a great way to start with your preferred characters. The game offers guest accounts and an easy account deletion feature, allowing for quick restarts without extensive re-downloads.

**Rerolling Process:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Login">
    Log in as a **guest** using the controller icon on the main screen. This avoids linking to a permanent account too early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fast Progression">
    Rush through the tutorial and initial story stages. Skip dialogue using the top-right button and run past non-boss enemies to save time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Rewards & Summon">
    Once summons are unlocked, go to your mail and claim all available rewards. This typically provides enough currency for several 10-pulls on both the standard and rate-up banners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Pulls">
    Check your pulls for desired 6-star characters, especially the rate-up character (e.g., Felgeia). Gold sparks during the summon animation indicate a 6-star.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Decide or Delete">
    If satisfied, link your account. If not, go to **Settings > Account > Account Deletion** and confirm to wipe your progress and start over.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The pity system guarantees a character after 80 pulls on a banner, allowing you to exchange tokens for a specific unit. Additionally, a beginner's benefit grants a 50-pull selector, letting you choose a companion after 50 draws.

<Callout type="success" title="Reroll Target Recommendation">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Summon Rates and Pity">
The gacha system in All Firing features two main banners: a rate-up banner for a featured character and a standard banner. Understanding the rates and the pity system is crucial for making informed decisions on where to spend your valuable summoning currency.

### Summon Rates Overview

| Banner Type     | Base Drop Rate (6-Star) | Overall Rate (with Guarantee) | Pity System (Exchange) |
| :-------------- | :---------------------- | :---------------------------- | :--------------------- |
| **Rate-Up Banner** | 1.2%                    | ~2.45%                        | 80 pulls for featured character |
| **Standard Banner**| 1.2%                    | ~2.45%                        | 80 pulls for any character |

<Callout type="warning" title="50/50 Chances">
On the rate-up banner, while the 6-star drop rate is 1.2%, there's typically a 50/50 chance to get the featured character if you do pull a 6-star. Be prepared for this possibility when aiming for specific units.
</Callout>

Each summon you perform on a banner grants you a specific token (rate-up token for the rate-up banner, standard token for the standard banner). These tokens accumulate towards the pity system.

### Pity System Mechanics

The pity system in All Firing is straightforward:

- **80 Pulls for Exchange**: After 80 pulls on either banner, you can exchange the accumulated tokens for a guaranteed 6-star character from that banner's pool. This means 80 rate-up tokens can be exchanged for the rate-up character, and 80 standard tokens for any standard banner character.
- **No Soft Pity**: Based on initial information, there doesn't appear to be a soft pity system (where rates gradually increase before the hard pity). It's a flat rate until you hit the 80-pull mark.

<Callout type="info" title="Beginner Selector">
New players receive a one-time special benefit: after 50 pulls on the standard banner, you gain a companion select opportunity, allowing you to choose one companion directly from a list. Beyond this, a companion selector is available every 300 draws on the standard banner.
</Callout>

### Initial Summon Strategy

For your initial rerolls, consider the following:

- **Target Rate-Up Characters**: Aim for the current rate-up character as they are often powerful DPS units that can carry your early game. While DPS units can eventually be replaced, having a strong one from the start is highly beneficial.
- **One Extra 6-Star**: Ideally, aim for the rate-up character plus at least one additional 6-star from your initial pulls.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Account Management and Troubleshooting">
Efficiently managing your account, especially during rerolls, involves understanding how to delete data and link your account once you're satisfied.

### Deleting a Guest Account

If your initial summons don't meet your expectations, deleting your guest account is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Settings">
    From the main screen, tap the top-left icon to open the settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Account">
    Within the settings, select the "Account" tab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Deletion">
    Choose "Account Linking," then "Account Deletion." You will be prompted to confirm your decision and type "Delete" to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return to Login">
    After confirmation, the game will return you to the home screen, allowing you to start a new guest account for another reroll attempt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permanent Deletion">
Account deletion is permanent and will erase all progress on that guest account. Only proceed if you are certain you want to reroll. Once you find an account you like, make sure to link it to a permanent login method (Google, Facebook, etc.) to save your progress.
</Callout>

### Linking Your Account

Once you've secured your desired characters, it's crucial to link your guest account to a permanent service:

- Navigate to the same "Account Linking" section in settings.
- Choose your preferred linking option (e.g., Google, Facebook).
- Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the linking process.

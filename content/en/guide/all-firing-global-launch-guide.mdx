A common mistake highlighted in this **all firing global launch guide** is the mismanagement of stamina (Energy). The game uses a stamina system for all material-grinding dungeons, and the costs are surprisingly high.

| Activity | Stamina Cost | Primary Rewards |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Warning">
With a total cap of 240 stamina and costs reaching 40 per run, you can deplete your daily allowance in just six battles. Many top players recommend using a portion of your premium currency for at least two stamina refreshes per day during the first week.
</Callout>

**Equipment and Relics:**
Relics function as your gear. They come in sets (2-piece and 4-piece). For example, a 2-piece Wind set increases Wind damage. Do not equip mismatched sets; the set bonuses are far more valuable than the individual base stats of higher-rarity gear in the early game.

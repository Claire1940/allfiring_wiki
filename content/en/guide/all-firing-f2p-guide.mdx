### Efficient Rerolling Strategy

The reroll process in All Firing is quite user-friendly. You can use guest accounts, complete the tutorial, perform your initial summons, and then delete the account data if you don't get your desired character. This typically takes around 8 minutes per attempt.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as Guest">
    Launch the game and select the guest login option. This allows for quick data deletion if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through Tutorial">
    Skip dialogues and run past enemies if possible to reach the summoning area faster. There's a skip button for dialogue in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, go to the mail icon and claim all rewards. This usually provides enough currency for several 10-pulls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Focus on the rate-up banner. Aim for the featured 6-star character (gold glow) or a strong 5-star character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Decide and Delete/Link">
    If satisfied, link your account. If not, go to Settings > Account > Account Deletion to restart the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Target">
As an F2P player, aim for the current **rate-up character** during rerolls. While DPS characters might be replaceable later, a strong early DPS significantly accelerates your initial progression.
</Callout>

### Elemental Affinity System

The elemental system in All Firing adds a layer of depth and strategy to combat. There are five main elements: Fire, Ice, Wind, Thunder, and Dark. Each element has specific strengths and weaknesses against others, making team composition crucial for challenging encounters. Pairing characters with compatible familiars and relics based on their element significantly boosts their effectiveness.

| Element  | Primary Focus                 | Typical Effects                             |
| :------- | :---------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ |
| **Fire** | High damage, Explosions       | Burning, Area of Effect (AoE) damage        |
| **Ice**  | Crowd Control, Slow           | Freezing, Slowing enemies                   |
| **Wind** | Agility, Flexible combat      | Rapid attacks, Movement buffs               |
| **Thunder** | Burst Damage, Flashy skills | Stun, High single-target damage             |
| **Dark** | Mysterious Powers             | Debuffs, Powerful unique effects            |

<Callout type="warning" title="Elemental Mismatch">
Avoid equipping familiars or relics with conflicting elemental damage types to your characters. For example, giving an Ice character a Fire-damage familiar will dilute your damage output and reduce overall efficiency. Always strive for elemental synergy.
</Callout>

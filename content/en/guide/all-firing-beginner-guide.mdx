<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Elemental Affinities">

Combat in ALLfiring revolves around a three-character squad. Unlike many gacha games where you use one character at a time, ALLfiring encourages constant switching. Tapping a character icon mid-combo allows the incoming unit to perform a "switch-in" attack, maintaining the flow of damage and allowing you to dodge incoming boss mechanics without losing your offensive pressure.

The elemental system adds a layer of strategy that prevents combat from feeling repetitive. There are five primary elements, each with specific combat characteristics:

| Element | Combat Style | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire** | Explosive / Heavy | High burst damage and AOE effects |
| **Ice** | Control / Slow | Freezing enemies and reducing movement speed |
| **Wind** | Swift / Flexible | High mobility and rapid combo potential |
| **Thunder** | Rapid / Flashy | High attack speed and burst skill chains |
| **Dark** | Mysterious / Utility | Powerful debuffs and crowd control |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not attempt to level up every character you pull. Upgrade materials are scarce in the early game. Select your best three characters and commit all your resources to them until they reach the level cap.
</Callout>

Furthermore, characters are divided into specific roles. A balanced team usually consists of one primary damage dealer and two units that provide utility or coordinated attacks.

- **Vanguard/Tank**: High survivability, stays on the front lines to soak up damage.
- **Striker/Assault**: Focused on dealing the highest possible damage in short windows.
- **Tactical/Strategy**: Provides buffs, field control, or debuffs to enemies.
- **Utility/Healer**: Keeps the team alive and cleanses negative status effects.
- **Coordinated Attack**: Specialized in off-field damage while other units are active.

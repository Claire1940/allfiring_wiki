### Character Switching and Combo Execution

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Your Team">
    Select three companions that complement each other's elemental types and roles (DPS, Support, Sub-DPS). Consider their linked elements for maximum synergy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Real-time Swapping">
    During combat, quickly switch between your active character and your two other companions to unleash their skills and abilities. This is key to maintaining pressure and adapting to enemy types.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combine Attacks">
    Utilize the brief moments after switching to chain attacks from different characters, creating powerful, uninterrupted combos. Experiment with various sequences to find the most effective combinations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Elemental Synergy">
    Pay attention to elemental links. For example, a support character like Garcia can boost damage for wind and ice elements, making her invaluable in specific team compositions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Combat Advice">
Ignoring the character switching mechanic will severely limit your combat potential. Effective switching is not just about bringing in a fresh character, but about leveraging elemental advantages and chaining abilities for massive damage.
</Callout>

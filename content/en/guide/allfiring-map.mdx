### Step-by-Step Exploration Guide
Follow these steps to ensure you're getting the most out of every ALLfiring map you encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Sweep: Main Path & Visible Collectibles">
    Begin by traversing the main path of the map, defeating enemies, and collecting any clearly visible items. This helps you understand the general layout and identify immediate threats. Don't worry about hidden areas just yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verticality Check: Climb & Drop Points">
    ALLfiring's voxel maps often feature multiple vertical layers. After clearing the main path, revisit areas to check for climbable walls, jumpable platforms, or hidden drop-down points. Many secrets are located on higher or lower levels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Block Breaking: Destructible Environments">
    Actively look for blocks that appear different or are slightly out of place. Many walls and floors can be destroyed to reveal hidden passages, secret rooms, or tucked-away chests. Use your attacks on suspicious blocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Corner to Corner: Check Every Nook">
    Once you've explored vertical aspects and broken obvious blocks, meticulously check every corner, dead-end, and small alcove. Hidden chests and rare materials can be cleverly tucked away in seemingly insignificant spots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Mini-Map Review: Unrevealed Areas">
    Regularly check your mini-map for any unrevealed or shaded areas. These often indicate sections you haven't fully explored or hidden rooms that are yet to be discovered. Mark these areas and investigate them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

In All Firing, efficient progression means making smart choices about where to invest your valuable resources. Spreading your resources too thin across many characters is a common mistake that can significantly slow down your progress.

### Prioritize Your Main Team

Focusing on a core team of three characters is the most effective strategy early on. These characters will be your primary damage dealers, support, and utility providers in most combat scenarios.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Core Trio">
    Choose three characters that complement each other in terms of element and role. A balanced team usually includes a damage dealer, a support, and a flexible third character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Concentrate Resources">
    Direct all your experience materials, upgrade items, and talent materials to these three characters. Avoid upgrading others until your main team is robust.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Talents and Engravings">
    Invest in your core team's talent trees and use duplicate character copies for Engravings to unlock powerful bonuses and stat increases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip Best Relics and Familiars">
    Ensure your main characters are equipped with the highest rarity relics (gear) that match their elemental type and provide set bonuses. Pair them with familiars that share their elemental affinity to boost effectiveness.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Synergistic Familiar Pairing">
Always pair familiars with characters of the same elemental type. A wind familiar on a wind character, for example, will maximize damage and utility, creating a powerful synergy in combat.
</Callout>

### Step-by-Step Reroll Guide

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as a Guest Account">
    Upon opening the game, select the guest login option (often represented by a controller icon on the far left). This allows you to play without linking an account, making data deletion easy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speedrun the Tutorial">
    Skip all dialogue and run past non-boss enemies whenever possible. The goal is to reach the summoning section as quickly as you can. You'll encounter an invisible wall if you can't proceed, indicating a mandatory battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, go to your in-game mail and claim all available rewards. This typically provides enough currency for several 10-pulls on both standard and rate-up banners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Execute your pulls on the rate-up banner first, then the standard banner. Look for gold animations, which signify a 6-star character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate and Decide">
    If you obtain your desired reroll target (e.g., the rate-up character), link your account. If not, proceed to delete the guest account data and restart the process from Step 1.
  </Step>

### Character Development Components

Characters in All Firing have several development paths:

*   **Attributes:** Leveling up your characters increases their core stats. Pay attention to elemental types (Fire, Ice, Wind, Thunder, Dark) as they provide damage advantages.
*   **Talents:** A talent tree allows you to upgrade different abilities, requiring materials found in the sanctum.
*   **Familiars:** These pets assist in battles. Crucially, equip familiars with the same element as your character to maximize damage output and elemental advantages.
*   **Relics:** This is your gear, offering stats and set bonuses. Always match relic set bonuses (e.g., wind damage increase) with characters of the corresponding element.
*   **Engravings:** Similar to awakenings, engravings use duplicate character copies to unlock additional bonuses and enhance abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as a Guest">

  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through the Tutorial">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Summons and Claim Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, navigate to the mail icon in the top left corner and claim all available rewards. This typically provides enough currency for multiple 10-pulls on both the standard and rate-up banners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Head to the summon banners. Your primary goal is the current rate-up 6-star character (e.g., Felgeia) and potentially an additional strong 6-star unit from the standard banner. Gold sparks during the summon animation indicate a 6-star character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate Your Pulls">
    Check your new characters. If you're satisfied with your pull (ideally the rate-up character plus another 6-star), link your account. If not, proceed to delete the guest account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Delete Account and Repeat">
    To delete your account, go to Settings -> Account -> Account Linking -> Account Deletion. Confirm the deletion. This will return you to the home screen without needing to re-download resources, allowing for quick repeat rerolls.
  </Step>

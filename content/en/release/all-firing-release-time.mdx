Here’s a step-by-step guide to efficiently reroll in ALLfiring:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with a Guest Account">
    Upon opening the game, select the controller icon on the far left of the login options. This allows you to play as a guest without linking an account, making data deletion easier later. You will receive a warning about losing progress if the guest account is deleted, but this is exactly what you want for rerolling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through the Tutorial">
    The tutorial takes approximately 8 minutes. During gameplay, you can often run past enemies if there's no invisible wall blocking your path. Utilize the skip button in the top right corner to bypass all dialogue and cutscenes, saving significant time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Perform Initial Summons">
    Once you unlock the summon feature, navigate to the mail icon in the top left corner and select "Claim All." This will provide you with a 10-pull for the standard banner and two 10-pulls worth of premium currency for the rate-up banner. You might also find extra currency in other in-game menus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Your Pulls">
    Look for gold sparks around the chest board during summon animations; this indicates a 6-star character. The current rate-up banner character, Felgeia, is a strong target. Ideally, aim for the rate-up character and at least one additional strong 6-star. The base drop rate for 6-star characters is 1.2%, with an overall rate of 2.45% including guarantees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reroll if Unsatisfied">
    If your pulls are not satisfactory, delete your account. Go to Settings > Account > Account Linking > Account Deletion. Confirm the deletion by typing "Confirm Delete Account." This will return you to the home screen, allowing you to start a new guest account without redownloading game resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Link Account (if successful)">
    If you achieve a desirable set of characters, link your guest account to a permanent login method (Google, Apple, etc.) to secure your progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

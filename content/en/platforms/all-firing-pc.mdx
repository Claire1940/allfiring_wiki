<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Summoning Mechanics and Pity System">

Understanding the summoning mechanics is vital for efficient resource management in ALLfiring PC. The game features a standard gacha system with a pity counter for both characters and weapons.

| Item Type | Pity Threshold | Cost per Multi-Pull | Notes                                                   |
| :-------- | :------------- | :------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Characters** | 80 Summons     | 10 Tokens           | Guaranteed 5-star character at 80 pulls.                |
| **Weapons**    | 24 Summons     | 10 Tokens           | Guaranteed 5-star weapon at 24 pulls.                   |
| **Total Hard Pity** | 104 Summons    | N/A                 | Combined pity for character and their signature weapon. |

<Callout type="info" title="Pity System Explained">
For every multi-pull (10 summons), you receive 10 currency tokens. These tokens contribute to the pity counter. Reaching 80 pity for characters or 24 pity for weapons guarantees a 5-star item. This means a hard pity for a character and their weapon could cost up to 104 total summons.
</Callout>

### Step-by-Step Reroll Guide

Follow these steps to efficiently reroll for your desired characters:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Guest Login">
    Start the game and choose the guest login option. This is typically represented by a controller icon on the far left of the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through Tutorial">
    Play through the initial tutorial as quickly as possible. Skip all dialogue using the button in the top right. Run past enemies if there's no invisible wall preventing further progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, navigate to the mail icon in the top left corner and claim all available rewards. This usually grants enough currency for at least one 10-pull on the standard banner and two 10-pulls on the rate-up banner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Go to the summon screen. Prioritize pulling on the rate-up banner for the featured character. 6-star characters are indicated by gold sparks around the chest board during the summon animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate Results">
    If you obtain your desired 6-star character (especially the rate-up character like Felgeia), consider linking your account. If not, proceed to delete the guest account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Delete Account and Repeat">
    To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Account Linking > Account Deletion. Confirm the deletion. This will return you to the home screen, allowing you to start a new guest account without re-downloading resources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

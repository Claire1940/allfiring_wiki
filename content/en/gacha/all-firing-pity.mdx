<Section icon={Swords} title="How the Pity System Works">
The core of the All Firing pity system revolves around collecting specific tokens from each banner. Every summon you perform on a banner will grant you one token corresponding to that banner. These tokens accumulate, and once you reach a certain threshold, you can exchange them for a guaranteed character from that banner's pool.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon on Desired Banner">
    Choose either the Rate-Up Banner or the Standard Banner based on the characters you wish to acquire. Each pull on these banners will award you one token.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accumulate Tokens">
    Continue summoning and accumulating tokens. The game clearly displays your current token count for each banner in the shop section.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Pity Threshold">
    Once you gather **80 tokens** for a specific banner, you reach the pity threshold. This signifies that you are guaranteed to obtain a 6-star character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exchange for Character">
    Navigate to the dedicated shop where you can use your 80 tokens to exchange for any available 6-star character from that banner's current pool. This allows you to specifically target characters you need for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Token Specificity">
Remember that tokens from the Rate-Up Banner are separate from those on the Standard Banner. You cannot mix and match tokens between different banners to reach pity. Plan your pulls accordingly to avoid wasting resources.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as Guest">

  <Step num="2" title="Speedrun Tutorial">

<Section icon={Target} title="Soft Pity, Hard Pity, and the 50/50 Mechanic">

To truly optimize your **all firing pity** strategy, it's vital to differentiate between soft pity, hard pity, and the 50/50 system. These elements combine to determine your chances of getting the desired character or weapon.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Soft Pity">
    - **Increased drop rates** for 5-star items.
    - Typically starts around 60-70 pulls on character banners.
    - Not explicitly stated in-game but observed by players.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hard Pity">
    - **Guaranteed 5-star item** after a specific number of pulls.
    - For character banners, this is usually 80-90 pulls.
    - For weapon banners, it's often slightly lower, around 70-80 pulls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="50/50 Mechanic">
    - When you hit a 5-star, there's a **50% chance** it's the featured item.
    - If you lose the 50/50, your next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured item.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The 50/50 mechanic is particularly important. If you pull a 5-star that is not the featured character or weapon, your next 5-star pull on the same type of banner is guaranteed to be the featured item. This "guaranteed" status carries over to future banners of the same type, making it possible to save up for a specific character even if you lose a 50/50 on a previous banner.

### Reroll Strategy for Banners

A strong start in All Firing can significantly impact your progression. Rerolling is a common strategy to ensure you begin with a desirable 6-star character from the banners.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as Guest Account">
    Launch the game and select the guest login option. This allows for quick data deletion if your initial pulls are unsatisfactory without affecting linked accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speedrun Tutorial">
    Follow the main story and skip dialogues to reach the summoning section as quickly as possible. You can often run past enemies in non-boss stages to save time. The tutorial summon guarantees a 6-star character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, go to the mail icon in the top left corner and claim all rewards. This typically provides enough currency for several 10-pulls on both the standard and rate-up banners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Prioritize the rate-up banner for a chance at the featured character, then use any remaining currency on the standard banner. Look for gold animations for 6-star units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate and Decide">
    If you pull the desired 6-star character (e.g., Felgia), link your account. If not, delete the guest account data via Settings > Account > Account Deletion and repeat the process. Rerolls can take as little as 8 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

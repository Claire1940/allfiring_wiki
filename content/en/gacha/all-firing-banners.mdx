The **all firing banners** utilize a token-based pity system rather than a traditional hidden counter. Every pull you perform grants you one specific token tied to that banner type. These tokens are spent in the exchange shop to directly purchase characters.

  <Card title="Standard Tokens">

| **Beginner Selector** | 50 Pulls | Choice of 1 Standard 6-Star | One-time |
| **Direct Exchange** | 80 Pulls | Featured/Selected Unit | Repeatable |

This system is particularly friendly to players who experience poor luck on the 1.2% base rate. By the time you reach 80 pulls, you have effectively "earned" the character regardless of your actual pull results.

<Callout type="info" title="Token Persistence">

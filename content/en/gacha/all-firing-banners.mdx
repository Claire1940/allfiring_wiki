### Key Banner Mechanics

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rate-Up Banners">
    - **Increased Drop Rates:** Feature specific 5-star characters or Light Cones with a significantly higher chance of being pulled.
    - **Limited Availability:** These banners run for a set period, after which the featured units may or may not return immediately.
    - **Pity System:** Often include a soft and hard pity, guaranteeing a 5-star unit after a certain number of pulls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Standard Banner">
    - **Permanent Pool:** Contains a wide array of 5-star and 4-star characters and Light Cones available at all times.
    - **No Rate-Up:** While you can get 5-star units, there's no increased chance for any specific one.
    - **Selector Option:** Some games offer a selector after a high number of pulls (e.g., 50 or 300 pulls) on the standard banner, allowing you to choose a specific unit.
  </Card>

Maximizing your pulls requires careful resource management and an understanding of the game's gacha mechanics. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you make the most of every summon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Your Current Roster">
    Before pulling, evaluate your team's strengths and weaknesses. Do you need more damage, support, or a specific elemental coverage? Identify the roles you need to fill. Avoid pulling for characters that don't significantly improve your team unless they are a personal favorite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Understand the Pity System">
    ALLfiring's gacha typically includes a pity system. For example, a 5-star character might be guaranteed after 80 pulls on a rate-up banner, with a 50/50 chance of getting the featured unit. If you lose the 50/50, the next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured one. Track your pity to plan your pulls efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Limited Banners">
    Limited-time rate-up banners often feature characters or Light Cones that are crucial for the current meta or have unique abilities. While the standard banner is always available, saving your premium currency for these limited banners is generally more efficient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save Premium Currency">
    Resist the urge to pull on every banner. Accumulate enough premium currency (Emberite, Stardust, etc.) to reach soft or hard pity for your desired unit. Spreading your resources too thin across multiple banners can leave you with no guaranteed 5-star.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Utilize Free Pulls & Events">
    Take advantage of all free pull opportunities, login bonuses, and event rewards. Pre-registering for the game, for instance, can grant valuable 6-star companions and other resources from day one. These resources can significantly boost your starting progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

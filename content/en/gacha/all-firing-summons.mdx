### Pity System Explained

All Firing incorporates a pity system to ensure players eventually receive rare items after a certain number of unsuccessful pulls. This system prevents endless bad luck streaks and provides a sense of guaranteed progression.

| Pity System | Description | Benefit | Strategy |
|:------------|:------------|:------------|:-------------|
| **Soft Pity** | Increased chance of rare drop after a certain number of pulls (e.g., 50 pulls). | Reduces the risk of long dry spells. | Track your pull count to anticipate soft pity. |
| **Hard Pity** | Guaranteed rare drop (e.g., 6-star character) after a fixed number of pulls (e.g., 100 pulls). | Ensures you will eventually get desired high-tier items. | Save currency for hard pity to guarantee specific units. |

### Relics and Engravings

Relics are essentially your gear, offering stats and set bonuses. Engravings act as awakenings, providing significant bonuses when you acquire duplicate characters through summons.

| Feature | Function | Impact on Character | Optimization Tip |
|:--------|:---------|:--------------------|:-----------------|
| **Relics** | Provide gear stats and set bonuses. | Boosts specific stats (e.g., wind damage) and offers unique effects. | Equip relics that align with your character's primary element and role. |
| **Engravings** | Obtained from duplicate character pulls. | Grants powerful passive bonuses and stat increases. | Prioritize engraving high-tier characters first to unlock their full potential. |

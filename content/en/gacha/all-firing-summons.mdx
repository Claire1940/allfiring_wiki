<Section icon={Target} title="The Reroll Process: A Quick Start Guide">
Rerolling is a common strategy in gacha games to begin with powerful characters. All Firing offers a relatively quick and user-friendly reroll process, thanks to its guest account system and data deletion option. This allows players to repeatedly attempt initial summons until they acquire their desired starting units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Guest Login">
    Upon launching the game, select the 'Guest' login option (often represented by a controller icon) to avoid linking a permanent account prematurely. This allows for easy data deletion later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speedrun Tutorial & Story">
    Advance through the tutorial and initial story segments as quickly as possible. Utilize the skip dialogue button in the top right corner and run past enemies if there are no mandatory boss battles to save time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim All Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, navigate to the mail icon in the top left corner and claim all available rewards. This typically provides enough premium currency for several 10-pulls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Use your accumulated currency on the desired banners. Many players target the rate-up character first due to their boosted drop chance. Look for gold animations for 6-star characters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate Results & Reroll">
    If you did not get your desired characters, proceed to delete your guest account data. Go to Settings > Account > Account Linking > Account Deletion. Confirm the deletion to reset your progress and start over from Step 1. If satisfied, link your account to a permanent service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Drop Rates and Pity Mechanics">
Understanding the probability of obtaining desired characters is crucial for any gacha game. All Firing employs a standard gacha model with specific drop rates for different rarities and a robust pity system to ensure players eventually get what they want.

<Callout type="info">
The base drop rate for a 6-star character is 1.2%, which is fairly standard for many gacha titles. The pity system significantly increases your chances over time.
</Callout>

The pity system in All Firing guarantees a 6-star character after a certain number of pulls. For both the rate-up and standard banners, this guarantee is typically met after 80 pulls. What's more, each summon grants you specific tokens that can be exchanged for characters in the shop, providing an alternative route to obtaining desired units.

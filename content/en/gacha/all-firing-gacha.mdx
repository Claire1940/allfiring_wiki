  <Step num="1" title="Guest Login">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through Tutorial">
    Play through the initial stages. Use the 'Skip' button in the top right for dialogue. In combat zones, if there is no invisible wall, you can run straight past enemies to reach the boss faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim Mail Rewards">
    Once you unlock the summon menu, go to the mail icon in the top left. 'Claim All' to receive your 10-pull for the standard banner and premium currency for roughly two additional 10-pulls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Summons">
    Spend your tickets on the Standard banner first, then use your premium currency on the Rate-Up banner (currently featuring Felgeia).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate or Reset">
    If you didn't get a 6-star, go to Settings > Account > Account Linking > Account Deletion. Type "Confirm Delete Account" to reset and start over instantly.
  </Step>

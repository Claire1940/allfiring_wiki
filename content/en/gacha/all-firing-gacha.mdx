Rerolling is a common strategy in gacha games to obtain desired characters early on without spending real money. All Firing offers a user-friendly reroll system, making it relatively quick and painless.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start as a Guest Account">
    Upon launching All Firing, select the guest login option. This allows you to reroll without linking a permanent account, saving you time and effort. Look for the controller icon on the bottom left of the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Speed Through the Tutorial">
    Skip all dialogue and cutscenes to expedite the tutorial. In battle, if there are no boss encounters, you can often run past enemies. An invisible wall will block you if you can't proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim All Mail Rewards">
    Once summons are unlocked, navigate to the mail icon (top-left corner) and claim all available rewards. This usually provides enough currency for at least one 10-pull on the Standard Banner and two 10-pulls on the Rate-Up Banner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Your Summons">
    Execute your pulls on both the Standard and Rate-Up banners. Watch for gold sparks during the summon animation, which indicate a 6-star character. Purple sparks signify a 5-star unit (SR equivalent).
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate Your Pulls">
    If you obtain your desired 6-star characters (ideally the rate-up DPS and another strong unit), link your account. If not, proceed to delete the guest account to start fresh.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Delete Account and Repeat">
    To delete your guest account, go to Settings > Account > Account Linking > Account Deletion. Confirm the deletion by typing "Confirm Delete Account". This will return you to the login screen, allowing for another reroll attempt without re-downloading resources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Account Deletion Warning">
Ensure you are on a guest account before attempting to delete. Deleting a linked account will result in permanent loss of progress. Always double-check your account status.
</Callout>

Executing the **all firing Sion** strategy effectively requires more than just good builds; it demands coordination and smart decision-making. Follow these steps to maximize your team's potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Lane Dominance">
    In the early game, focus on pushing your lane aggressively. Sion's Q, Decimating Smash, is a powerful tool for wave clear and poke. Coordinate with your lane partner to zone enemies and secure minion gold. Look for opportunities to land charged Qs on unsuspecting foes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Game Roaming and Objective Control">
    Once you hit level 6, your ultimate, Unstoppable Onslaught, becomes your primary engagement tool. Coordinate with your team to roam to other lanes or contest objectives like Dragon or Rift Herald. A well-timed five-man Sion ultimate can obliterate enemy teams and quickly secure objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chaining Ultimates for Relentless Pressure">
    The true power of all firing Sion lies in chaining ultimates. As one Sion hits an enemy with their ultimate, another should be ready to follow up. This keeps enemies stunned and unable to retaliate. Practice timing your ultimates to create a continuous lockdown effect.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Split Pushing and Team Fight Engagement">
    With items like Hullbreaker, one Sion can effectively split push while the others group. If a team fight breaks out, the split-pushing Sion can either join with their ultimate or continue pushing for a base race. In team fights, prioritize squishy targets and use your ultimate to disrupt their backline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Closing Out the Game">
    Once you have a significant lead, focus on securing inhibitors and ultimately the Nexus. Don't get complacent. Use your collective ultimate power to dive turrets and force favorable engagements. Keep the pressure high until the enemy Nexus falls.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Target} title="Ability Maxing Order and Summoner Spells">
Proper ability prioritization is key to maximizing Sion's impact in lane and throughout the game. For **All Firing Sion**, our focus is on his primary damage and wave clear tool, followed by his defensive scaling.

### Ability Order
<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Q - Decimating Smash">
    Max Q first. This is Sion's primary damage, wave clear, and crowd control ability. A fully charged Q can deal massive damage and knock up enemies, making it crucial for both farming and trades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="W - Soul Furnace">
    Max W second. Soul Furnace grants Sion a shield and allows him to deal magic damage in an area after the shield breaks or expires. More importantly, it grants permanent bonus health for every unit Sion kills while W is active, synergizing with his passive scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="E - Roar of the Slayer">
    Max E last. While useful for slowing enemies and shredding their armor, its damage and utility are less impactful than Q and W in the early to mid-game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="R" title="R - Unstoppable Onslaught">
    Put a point in your ultimate whenever available at levels 6, 11, and 16. This provides global presence, initiation, and escape potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Summoner Spells
| Spell | Purpose for Sion |
|:------|:-----------------|
| **Flash** | Essential for escaping dangerous situations, repositioning for a Q, or extending an R. |
| **Teleport** | Allows Sion to return to lane quickly, join teamfights across the map, or split-push effectively. |

<Callout type="info">
In some specific matchups, if you're against a highly aggressive melee champion, you might consider taking Ignite instead of Teleport for kill pressure, but Teleport is generally preferred for its global utility.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Tips and Tricks for All Firing Sion">
Beyond the core build, mastering Sion involves understanding his unique mechanics and how to leverage them for maximum impact.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Perfecting the Q Charge">
    Practice holding your Q (Decimating Smash) for the full duration to maximize damage and knock-up time. In lane, hide in bushes to charge Q unseen, catching enemies off guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="W Health Stacking">
    Always activate W (Soul Furnace) when farming minions or jungle camps to gain maximum bonus health. This passive scaling is a huge part of Sion's late-game tankiness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="E + Q Combo">
    Use E (Roar of the Slayer) to slow an enemy and shred their armor, making it easier to land a fully charged Q. This combo is excellent for trades and setting up ganks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ultimate Engages and Escapes">
    R (Unstoppable Onslaught) can be used for long-range engages, but also as an escape tool. Learn to steer it effectively to navigate terrain or escape chasing enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Post-Mortem Impact">
    Sion's passive, Glory in Death, allows him to continue fighting after being killed. Use this to secure kills, push towers, or even zone enemies in teamfights. Prioritize hitting enemies for life steal to extend your passive duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Use of R">
Don't be afraid to use your ultimate to quickly traverse the map, even if you don't hit an enemy. Its global presence can quickly turn a losing lane into a winning one or secure a crucial objective.
</Callout>

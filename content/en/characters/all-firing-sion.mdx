<Section icon={Target} title="Ability Maxing Order & Combos">

Proper ability leveling is crucial for Sion's early game and scaling into a true "all firing" threat.

### Ability Max Order

The standard and most effective ability maxing order for Sion Top is:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Max Q (Decimating Smash)">
    Prioritize Q first for its strong wave clear, poke, and burst damage. This allows you to control the lane and set up ganks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Max W (Soul Furnace)">
    After Q, max W for increased shield strength and base health gain. This significantly boosts your survivability in mid-game team fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Max E (Roar of the Slayer)">
    Max E last as its primary utility (slow and armor shred) doesn't scale as hard with points as Q and W.
  </Step>
  <Step num="R" title="Ult Whenever Possible">
    Put a point into your ultimate, Unstoppable Onslaught, whenever available at levels 6, 11, and 16.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Basic Combos

Mastering these basic combos will elevate your Sion gameplay:

-   **Lane Poke/Trade:** E (on minion/enemy) -> Q (charge for max damage/knock-up) -> W (shield for trade/damage).
-   **Engage/Teamfight:** R (Unstoppable Onslaught) into the enemy backline -> Q (fully charged knock-up) -> W (shield for damage absorption) -> E (for slow and armor shred on target).
-   **Post-Death:** Use your passive, Glory in Death, to chase down low-health enemies or deal significant damage to structures. Your Q and E abilities are enhanced in this state.

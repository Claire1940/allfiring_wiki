### How Companion Dismissal Works

The method for dismissing companions is consistent across all characters. You'll typically initiate a conversation with them on your ship, the *Unreliable*, and select a specific dialogue option.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Dialogue Options">
    - Approach the companion directly.
    - Initiate conversation.
    - Look for options like "I need you to leave the crew" or "I'm letting you go."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Immediate Effect">
    - Upon confirmation, the companion will leave your ship.
    - Their entry will be updated in your team index as "released from service."
    - Any active companion quests associated with them may be failed or put on hold.
  </Card>

### Can You Recruit New Companions?

If you decide to **fire all companions** but later regret it, the game does not offer a direct "re-recruit" option for previously dismissed crew members. However, you can still recruit any companions you haven't yet encountered in your playthrough, provided their recruitment quests are still available.

| Recruitment Status | Description                                |
| :----------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **New Recruits**   | You can still find and recruit companions you have not yet met. |
| **Dismissed**      | Companions you have explicitly dismissed are generally unavailable for re-recruitment. |
| **Story-Locked**   | Some companions might be temporarily unavailable due to story progression. |

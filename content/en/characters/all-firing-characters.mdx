### Familiar System

Familiars are your loyal pets that assist you in battles. They provide additional damage and effects, but their true power is unleashed when they are paired correctly with your characters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Familiar Elements">
    Just like characters, Familiars have elemental types (Fire, Ice, Wind). This is crucial for synergy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Match Familiar to Character">
    Always equip a Familiar whose element matches the character it's assigned to. For example, an Ice-element Familiar should go with an Ice-element character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Elemental Synergy">
    The combined elemental damage from a character and a matching Familiar provides a significant advantage against enemies weak to that element.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Stamina Management and Resource Grinding

The game features a stamina system used for grinding materials in dungeons. Deciding whether to spend premium currency on stamina or summons is a common dilemma for players.

| Resource Type    | Primary Use                                  | Acquisition Method                   | Stamina Cost | Grinding Efficiency Tip                                |
| :--------------- | :------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------- | :----------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |

| **Ascension Materials** | Character rank-up, advanced progression      | Specific Sanctum trials              | 40 per run   | Prioritize based on your highest-rarity characters.    |
| **Companion Talents** | Upgrading companion-specific abilities       | Familiar Grounds                     | 40 per run   | Grind these when you have excess stamina or specific needs. |

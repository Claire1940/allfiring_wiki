### Character Progression and Enhancement

To maximize your **All Firing character** potential, you'll need to invest in their growth through various systems. This includes leveling, upgrading talents, equipping Familiars, and utilizing Relics and Engravings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Leveling Up Characters">
    Gain experience through combat and story progression. Use experience materials to quickly boost character levels, unlocking higher stats and access to new talents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Talent Tree Upgrades">
    Each character has a unique talent tree. Spend materials (obtainable from the Sanctum) to upgrade abilities, enhancing their combat effectiveness and unlocking new skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equipping Familiars">
    Familiars act as pets, assisting in battles. Crucially, pair Familiars with characters of the **same elemental type** to maximize damage and gain elemental advantage. For example, an Ice Familiar with an Ice character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Relics (Gear) Acquisition">
    Relics are your character's gear, providing stats and set bonuses. Always aim for Relic sets that match your character's element to activate powerful bonuses, such as increased Wind damage for a Wind character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Engravings (Awakening)">
    When you obtain duplicate characters from the gacha system, you can use them for Engravings. These provide significant bonuses, such as automatic strength resonance stacks upon entering battle, further enhancing character power.
  </Step>
</Steps>

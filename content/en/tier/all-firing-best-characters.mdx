<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Little Drake">
    **Little Drake** stands out as the absolute best firing character, primarily due to his overwhelming close-range dominance. While he might be considered one of the weaker Imps in his class overall, his primary weapon's power up close is unparalleled. Coupled with the utility of his Z-Mech, which provides an additional health pool and explosive abilities, Little Drake can single-handedly turn the tide of close-quarters engagements.     <Callout type="success">

    </Callout>

  <Step num="2" title="Barbecue Corn">
    **Barbecue Corn** is a strong contender for the top spot, boasting one of the most explosive ability sets in the game. With Husk Hop, Shuck Shot, and Butter Barrage, he can deal massive area-of-effect damage. His primary weapon is powerful, and with 150 health, he has good survivability. Barbecue Corn is a well-rounded offensive character, capable of both consistent primary fire damage and devastating burst attacks.
    <Callout type="info">
    **Key Takeaway**: Barbecue Corn excels in group engagements and objective defense, where his area-clearing abilities can shine. Aim for clustered enemies to maximize his explosive potential.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Paleontologist">
    The **Paleontologist** is a unique support character who brings exceptional versatility. Unlike many scientists, he can be highly effective from mid to long range thanks to his splash damage combined with fire effects. This allows him to support his team and apply pressure from a safe distance, while still being a powerhouse up close. His ability to heal and revive teammates, coupled with consistent damage, makes him one of the most efficient support characters in the game.
    <Callout type="warning">
    **Consideration**: While highly versatile, Paleontologist's primary fire rate might feel slower compared to pure damage dealers. Focus on strategic positioning to maximize both damage and support.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

His ability set, though lacking the Hyper ability of Peashooters, provides a robust offensive presence. The added health compensates for his slightly lower mobility compared to characters like Fire Pea, making him a durable and impactful frontline fighter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fire Pea">

Despite being on the lower end of the Peashooter class in terms of maximum potential, his ease of use and consistent damage output make him a solid choice. His primary weapon applies fire damage effectively, and his Hyper ability grants him crucial mobility for aggressive plays and escapes.

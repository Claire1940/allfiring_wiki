### Rozeul: The Thunderous Main DPS

**Rozeul** is widely regarded as the cornerstone of many top-tier ALLfiring teams. His kit is a masterclass in consistency and versatility, allowing him to excel in almost any scenario.

**Why Rozeul is S-Tier:**
- **Element:** Thunder
- **Role:** Main DPS
- **Rarity:** 6★
- **Consistent Damage Output:** Delivers both fast AoE clearing and excellent single-target damage. This makes him effective against both hordes of enemies and powerful bosses.
- **Exceptional Flexibility:** Integrates seamlessly into mono-Thunder teams, hybrid setups, and even budget lineups without a drop in effectiveness. This adaptability is rare and highly valuable.
- **Low Investment, High Return:** Even without perfect gear or fully optimized teammates, Rozeul performs at a level that many other DPS units struggle to reach. He's a "safe investment" character.

### Sion: The Wind Utility Master

**Sion** is another indispensable S-Tier character, often described by players as the "glue" that holds teams together. Her utility and synergy with other top-tier units make her invaluable.

**Why Sion is S-Tier:**
- **Element:** Wind
- **Rarity:** 6★
- **Availability:** Free
- **Unmatched Utility:** Her Wind-element skills help maintain combat flow, allowing other DPS units like Rozeul to focus on their burst windows.
- **Low Investment Requirement:** Performs exceptionally well even with minimal investment, making her incredibly accessible for all players, especially F2P.
- **Team Synergy:** She makes almost every team composition smoother, enhancing the overall effectiveness of your squad.

<Section icon={Map} title="Progression Guide: How to Power Up Your Best Characters">
Once you have your dream team, the next step is to power them up effectively. This involves consistent grinding, smart resource management, and understanding the various upgrade systems.

### Character Development Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Leveling Up Characters">
    Utilize EXP materials to increase character levels. Higher levels unlock more base stats and talent points. Focus on your main DPS and support units first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Talent Tree Optimization">
    Access the talent tree for each character. Spend talent points and materials (obtained from the Sanctum) to upgrade abilities. Prioritize talents that boost elemental damage or core skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Familiar Pairing and Upgrades">
    Equip Familiars that match your character's element. Level up Familiars and their skills to provide additional damage and utility in combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Relic Acquisition and Enhancement">
    Farm for relics (gear) in dungeons. Look for set bonuses that align with your character's element and role. Enhance and upgrade relics to increase their stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Engraving for Awakening">
    Use duplicate character copies obtained from the gacha system for engravings. This "awakens" your character, granting significant stat bonuses and powerful passive effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management is Crucial">
Stamina is a limited resource and is very expensive in All Firing. It's often debated whether to use premium currency for stamina or summons. For long-term progression and material grinding, investing in stamina can be more beneficial than chasing summons, especially if you already have a solid core team.
</Callout>

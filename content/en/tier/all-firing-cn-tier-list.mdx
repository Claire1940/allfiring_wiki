<Section icon={Swords} title="T0 Characters: The Unrivaled Elite">
These characters represent the pinnacle of performance at ALLfiring's launch. They offer exceptional value, often excelling in multiple content types and requiring relatively low investment to be effective.

### Rozeul (Thunder, Main DPS, 6★)
Rozeul is considered a "safe investment" character due to his consistent performance across various game modes. His kit offers a potent combination of fast AoE clearing and strong single-target damage, making him invaluable for both exploration and boss encounters.

**Why Rozeul is T0:**
- **Consistent Damage:** Performs exceptionally well even with sub-optimal gear.
- **High Flexibility:** Fits into mono-Thunder teams, hybrid setups, and budget compositions.
- **Universal Value:** Useful in almost every type of content, from daily farming to challenging boss fights.

### Sion (Wind, Sub DPS, 6★)
Sion is a standout unit, not only for her performance but also because she is given to players for free. She acts as a crucial "glue" unit, providing essential Wind utility that smooths out combat flow and synergizes perfectly with other top-tier units like Rozeul.

**Why Sion is T0:**
- **Free Acquisition:** Obtainable without gacha pulls, making her accessible to all players.
- **High Utility:** Provides valuable Wind support, enhancing team performance.
- **Low Investment:** Performs effectively even at low investment levels, making her an excellent early-game asset.

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy Spotlight">
Pairing **Rozeul** with **Sion** creates a powerful and highly efficient core for many early-game teams. Sion's utility perfectly complements Rozeul's burst damage windows.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="T1 Characters: Strong & Situational Picks">
These characters are powerful additions to any roster, often excelling in specific roles or requiring moderate investment to shine. They might not be as universally flexible as T0 units but are still highly recommended.

### Ilscha (Ice, Burst DPS, 6★)
Ilscha is a controversial but incredibly potent unit. At base, she's a strong T1 DPS, but with her signature weapon and a dedicated Ice team, her damage potential skyrockets to near T0 levels. The main caveat is her high investment requirement.

**Ilscha's Investment Tiers:**

| Investment Level | Performance | Requirements | Notes |
| :--------------- | :---------- | :----------- | :---- |
| **Base Level** | Strong T1 DPS | Minimal | Good, but not exceptional. |
| **Mid-Tier** | High T1 DPS | Dedicated Ice teammates | Damage significantly improves with elemental synergy. |
| **Optimal** | Near T0 DPS | **Signature Weapon**, full Ice team | Unlocks her full burst potential, but very costly. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ilscha's Accessibility">
F2P players might find it challenging to fully unlock Ilscha's potential due to the need for her signature weapon and specific team compositions. Consider your resource allocation carefully before investing heavily.
</Callout>

### Felgia (Wind, Sustained DPS, 6★)
Felgia offers reliable sustained Wind damage, making her an excellent choice for prolonged battles. She synergizes well with Sion, forming a comfortable dual-Wind setup that is both safe and effective during progression.

**Felgia's Strengths:**
- **Sustained Damage:** Excels in longer fights where enemies don't fall in a single rotation.
- **Sion Synergy:** Enhances dual-Wind compositions, providing a stable foundation.
- **Low Investment:** Scales well even with average gear, making her a safe long-term investment.

### Kirika (Fire, DPS, 6★)
Kirika provides essential Fire-element coverage, excelling in content where elemental advantages are crucial, especially against Ice enemies. Her ability to slot into mixed teams without disrupting overall synergy makes her a practical choice.

**Kirika's Role:**
- **Elemental Coverage:** Dominates against Ice-element foes.
- **Team Flexibility:** Easily fits into diverse team compositions as a flex DPS.
- **Practical Value:** Fire damage remains consistently useful throughout the game.

<Section icon={Shield} title="T2 Characters: Niche & Beginner Options">
These characters offer utility or serve as serviceable options, especially for new players or specific niche situations. While not top-tier, they can still contribute to your team.

### Nayeh (Ice, 5★)
Nayeh is a decent 5-star Ice unit that can provide early-game support. While she won't carry your team in late-game content, she's resource-efficient and can fill a gap if you lack other Ice options.

### Herfwin (Dark, Tank/Bruiser, 5★)
Herfwin is a 5-star Dark tank/bruiser. In the current launch meta, aggressive, damage-focused teams are often favored over defensive setups, making Herfwin feel slower. However, for newer players struggling with survivability, he can be a surprisingly useful early-game option.

### Main Character (MC) (Adaptive, Flexible Starter)
The MC is designed as a beginner-friendly option. They are serviceable for clearing story content without major issues, but their effectiveness diminishes as stronger gacha units enter your roster. Most players eventually transition the MC into a backup utility role or cease heavy investment. The flexibility of adapting to different elements is a nice early-game perk.
